A bit about me...


My time spent as a Digital Content Creator opened my eyes to the link between programming and design, and encouraged me to enrol in General Assembly’s Software Engineering programme. Pac-Man


HTML, CSS, JavaScript


After finishing my coding course and beginning my role as teaching assistant at GA, I wanted to push myself further while my students began their first projects. I decided to build Pac-Man in Vanilla JavaScript and utilise my learnings from a very casual p5 course I was taking on Instagram TV. I decided I wanted my ghost movement to be random but had to work out the logic for only picking their new direction from the possible directions available to them (ie. not where a wall was placed). I added sound effects, music and a few extra features to add a personal touch.
